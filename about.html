The history of Yeovil Pizza Co. and a little bit about us

Yeovil Pizza Co. is a pizza restaurant located in Yeovil, a town in Somerset, England. The restaurant has become a popular destination for locals and visitors alike, offering a variety of delicious pizzas and other Italian-inspired dishes.

The history of Yeovil Pizza Co. can be traced back to the early 2000s, when a group of friends with a shared passion for food decided to open a small pizza place in the heart of Yeovil. They wanted to create a casual and friendly atmosphere where customers could enjoy fresh, handmade pizzas made with high-quality ingredients.

The restaurant quickly gained a following, with customers raving about the delicious pizzas and the welcoming atmosphere. Over time, Yeovil Pizza Co. expanded its menu to include other Italian dishes, such as pasta, salads, and desserts.

As the restaurant's popularity grew, so did its reputation. Yeovil Pizza Co. became known for its commitment to using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients, sourced from local suppliers whenever possible. The restaurant also prided itself on its friendly and welcoming staff, who went out of their way to make every customer feel at home.

Today, Yeovil Pizza Co. is a thriving restaurant that continues to attract a loyal following of customers. The menu has expanded even further to include vegetarian and gluten-free options, catering to a wide range of dietary needs. The restaurant has also embraced technology, offering online ordering and delivery services to make it even easier for customers to enjoy their favorite dishes.

Despite its growth and success, Yeovil Pizza Co. has never lost sight of its roots. The restaurant remains committed to its founding principles of using only the best ingredients and providing exceptional customer service. With its delicious pizzas, warm atmosphere, and commitment to quality, Yeovil Pizza Co. is sure to remain a favorite among pizza lovers for years to come.
